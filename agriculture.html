AGRICULTURE

The steepness of the Andes mountains additionally created many distinct biomes and environments in close proximity. This diversity in ecological environments enabled a unique agricultural strategy, where many different crops could be grown in a sort of vertical strip. Different sections of the mountain could host different kinds of crops and domesticated species.
